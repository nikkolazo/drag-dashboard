Does the company disclose financial risks related to waterway pollution and eutrophication from nutrient runoff? Please return NONE if there is no disclose at all, UNCLEAR if there is some mention of risk but no link to financial risk, PARTIAL if there is mention of financial risk, YES if there are numbers attached to financial risks related to human health problems.

Justification:
Nutrien acknowledges financial risks related to environmental matters, specifically an EPA enforcement initiative concerning its phosphate facilities and alleged violations of environmental laws. While penalties are deemed 'not material', the potential for capital improvement projects and financial assurance for phosphogypsum stack systems are identified financial implications. The company also reports 'accrued environmental costs' as a quantified liability.
